Ordering

How do I place an order?

Placing an order is easy! Simply browse our products by searching for your printer model, cartridge number, or browsing by category. Once you've found what you need, add items to your cart and proceed to checkout. Enter your delivery details and payment information to complete your order. You'll receive an email confirmation with your order details and tracking information once dispatched.

Can I change or cancel my order?

If you need to change or cancel your order, please contact us as soon as possible. We process orders quickly, so changes can only be made before your order is dispatched. Orders placed before 2pm are usually dispatched same day, so time is of the essence. Once shipped, you'll need to wait for delivery and follow our returns process. To make changes, call us on 0800 INK CART or email support@inkcartridges.co.nz with your order number.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept a wide range of payment methods for your convenience:

  1. Visa, Mastercard, and American Express credit/debit cards
  2. PayPal
  3. Afterpay (buy now, pay later in 4 interest-free instalments)
  4. POLi (direct bank transfer)
  5. Bank deposit (for approved business accounts)

All transactions are secured with SSL encryption for your protection.

Do I need an account to order?

No, you can checkout as a guest without creating an account. However, creating a free account offers several benefits:

  1. Faster checkout with saved addresses and payment methods
  2. Order history and easy reordering
  3. Track your orders online anytime
  4. Access to exclusive member discounts and offers
  5. Saved printer profiles for quick cartridge matching
Are prices in NZ dollars and inclusive of GST?

Yes, all prices displayed on our website are in New Zealand dollars (NZD) and include GST (15%). The price you see is the price you pay, with no hidden charges. Shipping costs are shown separately at checkout before you complete your purchase.

Returns & Refunds

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy on all products. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, you can return it for a refund or exchange provided:

How do I return an item?

To return an item, follow these simple steps:

Please do not send returns without first obtaining an RA number, as this may delay processing.

What if my item arrives damaged or faulty?

We're sorry if you received a damaged or faulty item. Please contact us immediately and we'll make it right. For damaged or faulty products, we will:

  1. Arrange free return shipping at no cost to you
  2. Send a replacement immediately, or process a full refund if you prefer

How long do refunds take?

Once we receive and inspect your return, refunds are processed within 3-5 business days. The refund will be credited to your original payment method:

You'll receive an email confirmation when your refund has been processed.

Products

What's the difference between genuine and compatible cartridges?

We offer both genuine (OEM) and compatible cartridges:

  1. Genuine/OEM: Made by the printer manufacturer (HP, Canon, Epson, Brother, etc.). These guarantee compatibility and are covered by the manufacturer's warranty. Ideal for users who prefer brand assurance.
  2. Compatible: Third-party cartridges designed to work with your printer. They offer significant cost savings (often 50-70% less) while delivering quality prints. Our compatibles are ISO certified and covered by our quality guarantee.

Both options provide excellent print quality. Choose genuine for brand assurance or compatible for value savings.

Are compatible cartridges safe to use?

Yes, our compatible cartridges are completely safe to use. They are manufactured to meet or exceed OEM specifications and undergo rigorous quality testing. Our compatible cartridges:

  1. Are ISO 9001 and ISO 14001 certified
  2. Use high-quality ink or toner formulations
  3. Include smart chips for accurate ink level reporting
  4. Are backed by our quality guarantee

Using compatible cartridges will not void your printer warranty under New Zealand consumer law.

What does "page yield" mean?

Page yield is the estimated number of pages a cartridge can print. This is measured using ISO/IEC standards based on 5% page coverage (a typical business document). Understanding page yield helps you:

  1. Compare value between different cartridge options
  2. Estimate how long a cartridge will last for your usage
  3. Calculate your cost per page

High-yield (XL) cartridges typically offer better value for high-volume printing. Actual yields may vary based on your print settings, document types, and page coverage.

How should I store unused cartridges?

To ensure optimal performance and shelf life, store your cartridges properly:

  1. Keep in original sealed packaging until ready to use
  2. Store at room temperature (15-25 degrees Celsius)
  3. Avoid direct sunlight and heat sources
  4. Store upright (not upside down or on their side)
  5. Keep away from dust and humid environments

Properly stored cartridges typically last 2-3 years from the manufacture date. Check the expiry date printed on the packaging.

Compatibility

How do I find the right cartridge for my printer?

Finding the right cartridge is easy with these methods:

  1. Search by printer model: Enter your printer model in our search bar (e.g., "HP OfficeJet Pro 8710")
  2. Search by cartridge number: If you know your cartridge code, search directly (e.g., "HP 952XL")
  3. Browse by brand: Select your printer brand and navigate to your specific model
  4. Check your current cartridge: The cartridge number is printed on the cartridge itself

Still unsure? Contact our team on 0800 INK CART and we'll help you find the perfect match.

What if I order the wrong cartridge?

Don't worry - we understand mistakes happen! If you ordered the wrong cartridge, you can return it for an exchange within 30 days, provided it's unopened and in its original sealed packaging. Contact us to arrange the exchange and we'll help you find the correct cartridge for your printer. If there's a price difference, we'll either refund or charge the difference accordingly.

Will a compatible cartridge void my printer warranty?

No, using compatible cartridges will not void your printer warranty. Under New Zealand's Consumer Guarantees Act, printer manufacturers cannot void your warranty simply for using non-OEM consumables. Your warranty remains valid as long as the cartridge itself didn't cause damage to the printer. In the extremely rare case that a compatible cartridge causes an issue, the cartridge manufacturer (not the printer manufacturer) would be responsible. Our compatible cartridges are thoroughly tested and backed by our quality guarantee.

My printer doesn't recognise the new cartridge. What should I do?

If your printer doesn't recognise a new cartridge, try these troubleshooting steps:

  1. Remove the cartridge and ensure all protective tape and seals have been removed
  2. Check that the cartridge is correctly and firmly seated in its slot
  3. Gently clean the cartridge contacts with a lint-free cloth
  4. Clean the printer's cartridge contacts
  5. Turn the printer off, wait 30 seconds, then turn it back on
  6. Try removing and reinserting the cartridge

If the issue persists after trying these steps, contact our support team for assistance. We'll help troubleshoot or arrange a replacement if needed.
