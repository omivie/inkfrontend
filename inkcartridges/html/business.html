Frequently Asked Questions

What are the requirements for a business account?

We require a valid NZBN or similar business registration. You'll need to provide your company details and a primary contact. Credit accounts require a credit check, but you can start with a prepaid account immediately.

How long does approval take?

Most applications are reviewed within 1-2 business days. Prepaid business accounts can be approved instantly. Credit account approval may take 3-5 business days depending on the credit check process.

Can I have multiple users on one account?

Yes! Business accounts can have multiple authorised users with individual logins. You can set spending limits and approval workflows for each user. This is perfect for organisations with multiple departments or locations.

What credit terms are available?

Subject to credit approval, we offer 30-day payment terms. Credit limits vary based on business size and history, typically starting from $500 to $10,000+. You can request a credit limit increase as your account history builds.
