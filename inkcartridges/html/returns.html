Returns & Refunds

Hassle-free returns within 30 days. Your satisfaction is our priority.

Our 30-Day Return Guarantee

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy for any reason, you can return most unused products within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange. No questions asked.

  • 30-day return window from date of delivery
  • Products must be unused and in original sealed packaging
  • Free returns for faulty or incorrectly supplied items
  • Refund to original payment method within 3-5 business days

What Can Be Returned

Eligible for Return

  • Unused ink cartridges in sealed original packaging
  • Unused toner cartridges in sealed original packaging
  • Unopened printer accessories and supplies
  • Printers in original unopened packaging (within 14 days)
  • Items received damaged or faulty
  • Incorrect items (we sent the wrong product)

Not Eligible for Return

  • Opened or used cartridges (unless faulty)
  • Products with broken seals or damaged packaging
  • Items purchased more than 30 days ago
  • Items damaged by misuse or improper storage
  • Clearance or final sale items (marked as non-returnable)
  • Bulk orders over 10 units (contact us for special arrangements)

How to Return an Item

Follow these simple steps to return a product:

  1. Step 1: Contact Us

    Email returns@inkcartridges.co.nz or call 0800 INK CART with your order number and reason for return. You can also submit a return request through your account dashboard if you have an account.

  2. Step 2: Receive Return Authorisation

    Within 1 business day, we'll send you a Return Authorisation (RA) number along with the return shipping address and any prepaid shipping labels (for faulty/incorrect items). Please wait for this email before sending your return.

  3. Step 3: Pack Your Return

    Securely pack the item(s) in the original packaging if possible. Include a copy of your order confirmation or packing slip. Write the RA number clearly on the outside of the package.

  4. Step 4: Ship Your Return

    Send the package to the address provided in your RA email. For change-of-mind returns, you are responsible for return shipping costs. For faulty or incorrect items, use the prepaid shipping label we provide.

  5. Step 5: Receive Your Refund

    Once we receive and inspect your return, we'll process your refund within 3-5 business days. You'll receive an email confirmation when the refund has been processed.

Important: Please do not send returns without first obtaining an RA number. Returns received without authorisation may be delayed or refused.

Refund Processing Times

Refunds are processed within 3-5 business days of receiving your return. The refund will be credited to your original payment method. Processing times vary depending on your payment method:

Payment Method Refund Processing Time
Credit/Debit Card (Visa, Mastercard, Amex) 3-5 business days after we process the refund
PayPal 1-2 business days after we process the refund
Afterpay Per Afterpay's refund policy (typically 1-3 business days)
POLi / Bank Transfer 2-5 business days after we process the refund
Business Account Credit Applied immediately to your account balance

Please note that the time for the refund to appear in your account depends on your bank or payment provider. If you haven't received your refund after the expected timeframe, please contact your bank first, then reach out to us if the issue persists.

Faulty or Damaged Items

If you receive a faulty, damaged, or incorrect item, please contact us immediately. We take quality very seriously and will prioritise resolving your issue.

For Faulty or Damaged Products, We Will:

  • Provide a prepaid return shipping label at no cost to you
  • Send a replacement immediately (or process a full refund if you prefer)
  • Priority process your case within 24 hours
  • Cover all shipping costs for the replacement

What We Need From You:

  • Your order number
  • Photos of the damaged product and packaging
  • A brief description of the fault or issue

Retain all original packaging until your replacement arrives or refund is processed, as we may request return of the faulty item for quality analysis.

Exchanges

Need to swap for a different product? We're happy to help with exchanges:

  • Same product, different quantity: We can adjust your order and refund or charge the difference
  • Different product: Return the original item and place a new order, or contact us to process the exchange
  • Wrong cartridge: If you ordered the wrong cartridge for your printer, we can help you find the right one and exchange accordingly

For exchanges, the same return conditions apply - items must be unused and in original sealed packaging. If there's a price difference between products, we'll either refund or charge the difference accordingly.

Your Consumer Rights

This return policy is in addition to your statutory rights under the New Zealand Consumer Guarantees Act 1993. Under this Act, products must:

  • Be of acceptable quality
  • Be fit for purpose
  • Match the description provided
  • Be safe and durable

If a product fails to meet these guarantees, you may be entitled to a repair, replacement, or refund depending on whether the failure is major or minor. We honour all your statutory rights and are committed to fair resolution of any issues.

For more information about your consumer rights, visit the Consumer Protection website.
