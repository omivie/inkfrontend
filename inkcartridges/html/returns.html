Returns & Refund Policy

Last updated: 5 April 2026

Overview

At InkCartridges.co.nz we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you are not happy with a product for any reason, you may return it within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange, subject to the conditions below.

This policy applies in addition to your statutory rights under New Zealand law, including the Consumer Guarantees Act 1993.

Eligibility for Returns

To be eligible for a change-of-mind return, the item must meet all of the following conditions:

  • The return is requested within 30 days of the delivery date
  • The product is unused and unopened in its original sealed packaging
  • The product packaging is undamaged and in resalable condition
  • You have your order number or proof of purchase

Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned for change-of-mind reasons:

  • Products that have been opened, installed, or used
  • Products with broken or missing seals
  • Products returned after the 30-day return window
  • Products that have been damaged by the customer
  • Clearance or final sale items (marked as non-returnable at the time of purchase)

Note: These exclusions do not apply to defective, faulty, or incorrectly shipped products, which are always eligible for a remedy under the Consumer Guarantees Act 1993.

Defective or Incorrect Products

If you receive a defective product or a product that is different from what you ordered, we will:

  • Replace the product at no extra cost, or
  • Issue a full refund including any shipping costs you paid

Defective product claims may be made at any time within the manufacturer's warranty period. There is no time limit for claims under the Consumer Guarantees Act. Please contact us as soon as you discover the issue.

How to Request a Return

  1. Contact us by email at inkandtoner@windowslive.com or phone 027 474 0115 with your order number and reason for the return.
  2. We will issue a Return Authorisation (RA) number within 1 business day. Please wait for this before sending your return.
  3. Pack the item securely in its original packaging if possible, and write the RA number on the outside of the package.
  4. Ship the package to the return address provided. For faulty or incorrect items, we will provide a prepaid return label at no cost to you.
  5. Once we receive and inspect the returned item, we will process your refund or exchange.

Important: Please do not send returns without an RA number, as this may delay processing.

Return Shipping

  • Change-of-mind returns: The customer is responsible for return shipping costs.
  • Defective or incorrect products: We will cover return shipping costs or arrange a prepaid return label.

We recommend using a tracked shipping service for all returns. InkCartridges.co.nz is not responsible for items lost in return transit where no tracking is used.

Refunds

Refunds are processed to the original payment method used for the order.

Processing Times

  • Credit/Debit Card: 3–5 business days after we process the refund
  • PayPal: 1–2 business days after we process the refund

The time for the refund to appear in your account may vary depending on your bank or payment provider.

What Is Refunded

  • Refunds include the full product price (including GST)
  • Original shipping fees are only refunded for defective, faulty, or incorrectly shipped products
  • For change-of-mind returns, the original shipping fee is not refunded

Exchanges

If you would like to exchange a product for a different item (e.g., you ordered the wrong cartridge model), please contact us within 30 days and we will arrange the exchange. The original product must be unopened and in resalable condition.

  • Same-value exchanges: We will ship the replacement once we receive your returned item. You are responsible for return shipping costs.
  • Different-value exchanges: If the replacement item is a different price, we will refund or charge the difference accordingly.

Your Rights Under the Consumer Guarantees Act 1993

Nothing in this policy limits your rights under the New Zealand Consumer Guarantees Act 1993 (CGA). Products sold to consumers must:

  • Be of acceptable quality
  • Be fit for their intended purpose
  • Match their description on our Website
  • Be safe and durable

If a product fails to meet these guarantees:

  • Major failure: You may choose a full refund or a replacement. A major failure is one where the product would not have been purchased by a reasonable consumer if they had known about the problem, the product is substantially unfit for its normal purpose, or the product is unsafe.
  • Minor failure: We may choose to repair or replace the product within a reasonable time. If we do not do so, you are entitled to a refund or replacement.

These rights apply regardless of our return policy timeframes and cannot be excluded or limited.
