Shipping Information

Last updated: 19 April 2026

Overview

We deliver across New Zealand using NZ Post and overnight courier services. Orders placed before 2pm on business days are dispatched same day. Prices below include GST and are charged at checkout based on your delivery address and order weight.

Free Shipping on Orders Over $100 NZD

All orders totalling $100 or more (after any discounts, inclusive of GST) qualify for free standard shipping to any New Zealand address — urban or rural, North or South Island, no exceptions.

How Product Weights Work

  • Ink cartridges and ribbons: 100g per unit
  • Toner cartridges, drum units, and fuser kits: 2kg per unit
  • Multi-packs: weight × number of units (e.g. twin-pack ink = 200g)
  • Colour packs (CMY): 300g | Colour packs (KCMY/CMYK): 400g

Shipping cost is calculated automatically at checkout once your address is entered.

Dispatch & Delivery

Dispatch times

Orders placed before 2pm Monday–Friday are dispatched same business day. Orders placed after 2pm or on weekends/public holidays are dispatched the next business day.

Tracking

A tracking number is emailed to you once your order is dispatched.

Rural delivery

Rural addresses (as defined by NZ Post) attract a rural surcharge. The checkout will automatically detect your address type and apply the correct rate.
