3. Orders and acceptance

Adding an item to your cart and clicking “Place Order” is an offer to buy from us. Your offer becomes a contract once we send you an order-confirmation email with an order number. If we can’t accept your order — for example because of a stock, pricing, or fraud check — we’ll let you know within one business day and refund any payment in full to the original payment method.

We may decline or cancel orders that look like resale-for-profit volumes, show signs of fraud, or ship to addresses we can’t verify. Where we cancel a paid order on those grounds, you get a full refund — no admin fee.

4. Pricing, GST, and no hidden fees

All prices are in NZD and include GST at the New Zealand rate. The price you see on the product page is the price you pay for that item.

No hidden fees

We don’t add card surcharges, “handling” fees, or “processing” fees. The total at checkout is the total you’ll be charged. Shipping is shown before you pay; orders of $100 or more ship free anywhere in New Zealand.

We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay. Card payments are processed securely by Stripe; PayPal payments by PayPal. We never see or store your full card number.

We do our best to keep prices accurate. If a pricing error is obvious — for example an item priced at NZD$1 that should clearly be NZD$100 — we’ll contact you before fulfilling the order. You can either pay the corrected price or get a full refund; we’ll never quietly charge you more than the price you accepted.

5. Stock and availability

We list real-time stock on every product page. Where an item is out of stock, the buy button changes to Contact us so you can ask about restock dates. We don’t hold stock at our Auckland office — every order is dispatched directly by one of our New Zealand wholesale supplier partners on our behalf, through the same NZ Post / Aramex networks any local retailer uses. A small number of low-velocity SKUs add 1–2 working days to handling and that lead time is disclosed on the relevant product page. See the Shipping & Delivery Policy for the full mechanics.

7. Returns, refunds, and the Consumer Guarantees Act

Our full Refund & Return Policy sets out how we handle change-of-mind returns, faulty goods, and incorrectly supplied items. The short version:

  • Faulty, damaged, or not as described: we replace, repair, or refund — your choice for major failures, our choice for minor ones, exactly as the Consumer Guarantees Act requires. There is no time limit on this — these rights apply for as long as is reasonable for the goods.

  • Change of mind, unopened: within 30 days we refund the item price; you cover return shipping. Cartridge and toner boxes must be unopened with the manufacturer’s seal intact.
