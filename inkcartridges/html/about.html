About InkCartridges.co.nz

New Zealand's trusted source for quality printing supplies since 2015

Our Story

InkCartridges.co.nz was founded with a simple mission: to provide New Zealand businesses and homes with quality printing supplies at fair prices, backed by genuine Kiwi service.

What started as a small operation in Auckland has grown into one of New Zealand's most trusted online suppliers of ink cartridges, toner, and printing accessories. We've built our reputation on quality products, honest pricing, and customer service that actually cares.

Today, we serve thousands of customers across New Zealand, from home offices to large corporates, schools to government departments. Our commitment remains the same: making printing supplies simple, affordable, and hassle-free.

Why Choose Us?

Genuine & Compatible Options

We offer both genuine OEM and quality compatible cartridges, so you can choose what's right for your needs and budget. Our compatible cartridges are carefully selected and tested to ensure reliable performance.

Fast NZ-Wide Delivery

Orders placed before 2pm are dispatched same day. Most NZ addresses receive their order within 1-3 business days. Free shipping available on orders over $100.

Expert Product Knowledge

Not sure which cartridge you need? Our team knows printers inside-out. We can help you find the right product, every time. Just give us a call or send a message.

Business Account Benefits

For businesses, schools, and organisations, we offer credit accounts, volume discounts, and dedicated support. Join hundreds of NZ businesses who trust us for their printing supplies.
