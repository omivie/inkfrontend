A small Auckland team that takes printer ink seriously.

InkCartridges.co.nz is 100% New Zealand owned and operated. We supply genuine and compatible printer ink, toner, and typewriter ribbons to homes, schools, and businesses across the country — coordinated from our Auckland office and dispatched directly by our New Zealand wholesale supplier partners, usually within two working days.

1. Why we’re here

Most New Zealand printer-supply websites are either offshore marketplaces with three-week shipping, or local stores charging that for a single black ink. We started InkCartridges.co.nz because there was an obvious gap: a New Zealand business that stocks the cartridge you actually need, ships it tracked the same day, charges a fair price, and answers the phone when you call.

We’re a small team. The person who packs your order today is the same person who replies to your email tomorrow. That keeps us honest about stock, careful about quality, and very reluctant to leave a customer waiting.

3. How we work

We’re a small team running this business from an Auckland office — the same address you can see on the map below. We don’t hold stock ourselves; we partner with established New Zealand wholesale suppliers who hold the cartridges and dispatch them directly to you on our behalf. This is sometimes called “dropshipping” and we want to be upfront about it: it’s the model that lets a small Kiwi team carry every brand and yield without the markup of a big-box retailer.

What it means for you: orders are picked and packed by a New Zealand supplier, shipped on the same NZ Post / Aramex networks any local retailer uses, and arrive in the same time window. Tracking, GST receipts, and returns all flow through us. See the Shipping & Delivery Policy for the full mechanics.

Customer support, returns, refunds, and warranty all run through us — never through a supplier and never through an offshore call centre. One number, one inbox, one team.

5. The environmental bit

Every empty cartridge we receive back goes into a manufacturer recycling stream (HP Planet Partners, Brother Environmental Programme, Canon’s recycling pickup, etc.) — never to landfill. Every order ships with a pre-printed return envelope you can use to send empties back to our office at our cost; we batch them up and pass them to the relevant manufacturer programme. If you’re local to Auckland and would rather drop them off, call ahead and we’ll arrange a time at the office.

6. Call or write

Our office is small and not staffed for retail walk-ins — please call or email first if you’d like to drop in. We pick up the phone in business hours and reply to email within one working day, every time.
