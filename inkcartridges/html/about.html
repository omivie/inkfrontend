A small Auckland team that takes printer ink seriously.

InkCartridges.co.nz is operated by Office Consumables Ltd, an NZ-registered company based in Kelston, Auckland, supplying printer cartridges since 2008. We ship genuine and compatible printer ink, toner, and typewriter ribbons to homes, schools, and businesses across the country on the NZ Post and Aramex courier networks, usually within one to two working days.

1. Why we’re here

Most New Zealand printer-supply websites are either offshore marketplaces with three-week shipping, or local stores charging that for a single black ink. We started InkCartridges.co.nz because there was an obvious gap: a New Zealand business that stocks the cartridge you actually need, ships it tracked the same day, charges a fair price, and answers the phone when you call.

We’re a small team. The same person who replies to your email is the one chasing it up with the supplier on your behalf. That keeps us careful about quality and very reluctant to leave a customer waiting.

2. What we believe

100% NZ owned

Our company, our team, our office, our phone number, our payments — all here in New Zealand. Your money stays in the local economy.

Same-day dispatch

Auckland metro orders placed before 2pm on a business day are dispatched same-day; metro typically has the parcel the next morning. Outside Auckland metro, dispatch is the next working day.

Real support

Call 027 474 0115 in business hours and a person picks up. Email replies within one business day, every time.

Buy once, prints right

Genuine cartridges back the manufacturer warranty. Compatibles are covered by our 30-day satisfaction guarantee. Never “chip-failed” surprises.

Fair pricing, no surcharges

All prices in NZD include GST. We never add card surcharges or “handling fees” at checkout. Free shipping over $100.

3. How we work

We’re a small team running this business from an Auckland office — the same address you can see on the map below. Orders ship across New Zealand on the NZ Post and Aramex courier networks. Auckland metro orders placed before 2pm on a business day are dispatched same-day; outside Auckland metro, dispatch is the next working day. A small number of low-velocity SKUs add 1–2 working days, and that lead time is disclosed on the relevant product page.

Tracking, GST receipts, and returns all flow through us. See the Shipping & Delivery Policy for handling times, courier zones, and rates.

Customer support, returns, refunds, and warranty all run through us — never through an offshore call centre. One number, one inbox, one team.

4. What we stock

We carry two kinds of cartridges, each clearly labelled on every product page:

  • Genuine (OEM): made by the printer manufacturer — HP, Brother, Canon, Epson, Lexmark, Samsung, Kyocera and several others. Backed by the manufacturer’s warranty.
  • Compatible: third-party cartridges built to comparable specifications, offered at a lower cost than genuine. Each one is quality-checked on receipt and covered by our 30-day satisfaction guarantee.

We never blur the line between the two. Browse the full range →

5. Where we ship

Every order is dispatched from our Kelston, Auckland office using NZ Post and Aramex (CourierPost). All parcels are tracked end-to-end.

  • Auckland metro — 1–2 working days from dispatch.
  • Rest of North Island — 1–3 working days from dispatch.
  • South Island — 2–4 working days from dispatch.
  • Rural delivery & NZ public-holiday weekends add 1–2 working days.

Auckland-metro orders placed before 2pm NZT, Auckland metro, business days are dispatched the same day. Full carrier rates and rural-rural surcharges are documented on the Shipping & Delivery page.

7. Consumer rights

Your statutory rights under the New Zealand Consumer Guarantees Act 1993 are unaffected by your choice of cartridge — the CGA applies to every product we sell, genuine and compatible alike. We sell in trade, so we cannot contract out of the guarantees for consumer purchases.

Full detail on how returns, refunds, and warranty work — including the 30-day window for faulty / damaged / incorrectly-supplied items and the 30-day change-of-mind window for unopened cartridges — is in our Refund & Return Policy and Terms of Service.
