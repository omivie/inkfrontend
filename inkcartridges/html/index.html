Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What's the difference between genuine and compatible cartridges?

Genuine (OEM) cartridges are made by the printer manufacturer (e.g. HP, Canon, Brother). Compatible cartridges are made by third-party manufacturers to the same specifications. Both work in your printer — compatible cartridges simply offer a more affordable option without compromising on quality.

How do I find the right cartridge for my printer?

Use our "Find Your Ink Fast" tool above — select your printer brand, series, and model, and we'll show you every compatible cartridge. Alternatively, you can search by the cartridge code printed on your current cartridge. If you're still unsure, contact us and we'll help you find the right ink.

How long does delivery take?

Orders placed before 2pm are dispatched the same business day. Most NZ addresses receive their order within 1-3 business days. Rural deliveries may take an extra day. Shipping is free on orders over $100; otherwise a flat $5 fee applies.

What is your returns policy?

We offer a 30-day returns policy on unused, unopened products. If a product is faulty or doesn't work as expected, we'll replace it at no cost. Simply contact our support team to arrange a return.

Will compatible cartridges void my printer warranty?

No. Under New Zealand consumer law, using compatible cartridges does not void your printer warranty. Printer manufacturers cannot require you to use only their branded cartridges.
