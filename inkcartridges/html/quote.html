        Quote questions

        Do I need to know my cartridge code?

        No. A photo of your printer’s model label or cartridge box, or just the printer’s make and model, is enough — we’ll match the right supplies for you.

        Can I upload my current supplier invoice?

        Yes — a recent invoice or purchase list (PDF, spreadsheet, or photo) is often the fastest way to an accurate like-for-like quote. A team member reviews every upload personally.

        Can you quote genuine and compatible options?

        Yes. Ask for either — or both side by side — per product. Compatible cartridges offer significant savings; genuine keeps you on the manufacturer’s own supplies.

        How quickly will I receive a response?

        A real person reviews your request and replies within one business day (Mon–Fri, 9am–5pm NZT). If it’s urgent, call 027 474 0115.

        Do I need an account to request a quote?

        No account needed — just a name, business, and email address so we can send your pricing.
